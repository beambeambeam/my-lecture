# Torque & Static Equilibrium

$$
F_{net}=ma \to a=0 \text{ then } F_{net}=0
$$
$$
\tau_{net}=I\alpha\to \alpha=0\text{ then } \tau_{net}=0
$$

When in Equilibrium
$$
\begin{align}
\sum F_{x} & =0 \\
\sum F_{y} & =0 \\
\sum \tau & =0
\end{align}
$$
