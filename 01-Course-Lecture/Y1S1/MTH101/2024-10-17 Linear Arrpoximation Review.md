# Linear Approximation

$$
y=f(a)+f'(a)(x-a)
$$

![[Pasted image 20241017223356.png]]

$\therefore f(x) \approx \sqrt{ 1+0 } \approx 1$

![[Pasted image 20241017223526.png]]

$$
\begin{align}
y & =\sqrt{ 1 }+ \frac{1}{2\sqrt{ 1 }}(1.1-1) \\
 & =1+\frac{1}{2}(0.1) \\
 & =\frac{20}{20}+\frac{1}{20} \\
 & = 1.05
\end{align}
$$
